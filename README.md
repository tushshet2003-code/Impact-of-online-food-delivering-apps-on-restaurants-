# Impact-of-online-food-delivering-apps-on-restaurants-
  Food-delivery-impact-restaurant-study

# Project overview 
> A primary research study analyzing the impact of online food delivering apps i.e zomato and swiggy on 22 restaurants situated in Hubli city.
> Duration: June 2024 - August 2024
  Type: Academic research project.
  Tools used: Microsoft excel | MS Word
|
# Objectives 
> Understand patterns of how food delivery apps affected the restaurants.
> Identify key business trends and challenges faced by restaurant owners.
> Generate actionable insights for marketing strategies & customer targeting.
|
# Methodology
> Designed and executed a primary research survey using kobo collect app which icluded a questionnaire of 10 questions.
> Collected data from multiple restaurants across Hubballi city
> Interacted with the owners, managers of the restaurants and collected structured data.
|
> # What I did?
>  Cleaned and organized raw survey data in Microsoft Excel.
>  Analyzed data to surface key business trends and patterns.
>  Prepared a comprehensive findings report with actionable recommendations.
>  Presented insights and business implications to faculty.
|
# Key insights 
> During the field work, it was noticed different opinions. Some of then resaturants were not registered because they had too much of offline customers to handle.
> Some also commented on the delivery boys and their behaviour, some also told about the customers of how they should be patient and considerate.
> Got to know the weekly and monthly expenditures of the restaurants and how they manage the crowd adn online orders together.
